Profile

Bridging Academia & Industry

Senior academic leader with 28+ years spanning NIT Trichy, government-funded programmes, and industry. As Deputy Dean at Sricity International University, Dr. Sathishkumar architects academic programmes, drives accreditation strategy, and builds the research ecosystem for the School of Software Technology & AI.

A specialist in NBA, NAAC, ISO, and AICTE accreditation processes, he has consistently delivered measurable outcomes across roles as Deputy Dean, HOD, Placement Director, and State Head. His pioneering work in Problem-Based Learning — trained at Aalborg University, Denmark — has transformed curriculum delivery at multiple institutions.

He successfully managed MHRD-funded DDU-GKY & PMKVY projects worth ₹9 Crores across Tamil Nadu, delivering large-scale skill development programmes that directly impacted thousands of students and communities.
